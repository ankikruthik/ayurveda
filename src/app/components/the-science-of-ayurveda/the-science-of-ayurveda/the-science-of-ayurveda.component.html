The Science of Ayurveda

Ayurveda is a natural form of medicine which originated in India more than 3000 years ago. It comes from the Sanskrit words Ayur- life and veda- science making Ayurveda, the science of life. Ayurveda strives to find balance between the mind, the body and your environment. This balance allows the body to be in its healthiest state. This balance is reached through an intricate blend of herbs which work to eliminate impurities, reduce stress, increase they body's defenses and in general improve general wellbeing.
